About Me

Howdy ya'll! My name is Nathan Cochran and I am a music enthusiast. This website is dedicated towards helping you learn to sing parts in harmony with one another--just listen and sing along!

The Hymns section of this website lets you search for songs and listen to the parts of the harmony either individually or together. Check out my Youtube Channel for tutorials, challenges, and hymns.
